CONCEPT

Δ-AI is a novel educational package aimed at teaching anyone how to program while educating you in a more general sense in Science.

The information from the scientific field of AI can be used as a tool to teach students about learning and thinking in general: simultaneously introducing how the brain works and how to think better. All of this while teaching people the languages of the future: programming languages. The trifecta-AI approach, or Δ-AI, is then designed considering well established cognitive facts, learning-theory and Artificial Intelligence. The field of AI studies intelligence and knowledge in a very general sense. The underlying theory of the field is invaluable to any person that wants to learn anything. As an educational architecture, Δ-AI reverts back to basics. Before anyone is taught anything they should be taught what it means to learn, how the brain learns and what are the optimal learning strategies - this is the virtue behind Δ-AI: knowing how things can be known. This means subjects like Computational Theory, Logic, Mathematics and Programming are intentionally emphasised in the material. An applied flavour of these fields is taught explicitly as a tool to tackle real life problems by explicitly teaching student's how their own brains work. This is also made possible through incentivising critical thinking (i.e. the analysis of available facts, evidence, observations, and arguments to form a reasonable judgment).
